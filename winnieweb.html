About Me

I'm a Software Developer

I’m a career-switching software developer with over a decade of experience managing large-scale programmes, financial workflows, and grant management systems across national cultural organisations. In my previous roles, I oversaw funding portfolios, configured CRM platforms such as Blackbaud and Flexigrant, and worked closely with finance teams to ensure accurate data processing, compliance, and reporting.

Through this work, I became increasingly interested in the systems behind the operations, specifically, the logic, structure, and data architecture that power complex financial processes. That curiosity led me to formally train in Python, JavaScript, cybersecurity, and data management, and to begin building software applications.

I'm comfortable breaking down complex problems, mapping user journeys, and thinking carefully about backend logic, data validation, and workflow efficiency. My background means I don’t just focus on writing code, I think about how software functions within real organisations, under real constraints, serving real users.

I’m particularly interested in financial systems, fintech, and backend development, where precision, security, and scalability matter. My goal is to build reliable, well-structured applications that improve operational efficiency and create equitable, accessible digital experiences.
